DB Connectivity - Drivers, Clients etc.:
Once the DB gets created and populated with your data, you will be able to use data analisys tool of your choice to access the database/data and perform the analisys or manipulate the data. There are currently no restrictions on the connectivity methods and you should be able to use anything that PostgreSQL databases support out of the box. I have tested mine with raw SQL, Plotly/Dash, MS PowerBI (ODBC), MS Excel(ODBC) and Tableau (ODBC).
